My New York Times Collection

Instructions: There are six buttons below, each representing a ajax call to the server to retrive certain information.

"Text Articles" is used to retrive all articles with their titles, abstracts, published dates, and short links.

"All Authors" is used to retrive a list of all known authors.

"All URLs" is used to retrive a list of known urls linked to articles. These urls are grouped by published dates.

"All Tags" is used to retrive all the tags, displaed in the form of tag cloud.

"Article" is used to retrive detailed information about a specific article. Type in the desired article index first.

"Media Articles" is used to retrive all articles in the form of hyperlinked multimedia. It could be a video or an image.
